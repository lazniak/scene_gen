# Prompt Instrukcja dla Scene Gen (do pola prompt_instruction)

Skopiuj poniższy tekst i wklej go do pola `prompt_instruction` w węźle Scene Gen, aby wygenerować teledysk do piosenki z Suno.

---

**Prompt:**

> Create a high-energy, dynamic music video for a hip-hop tutorial track about AI video generation.
>
> **Visual Style:** "Tech-Hop" aesthetic. A seamless blend of gritty 90s street culture (graffiti, boomboxes, fisheye lens shots, vinyl textures) and futuristic Cyberpunk UI design (holographic nodes, glowing data cables, floating code, glassmorphism).
>
> **Key Visual Elements:**
> 1.  **The Workspace:** Visualize the software interface as a 3D immersive cyberspace where "Nodes" are physical, glowing neon boxes connected by vibrating laser wires.
> 2.  **The AI Entity:** Represent the AI (Gemini) as a futuristic, holographic DJ or a glowing digital brain that pulses to the beat, analyzing floating audio waveforms.
> 3.  **Data Flow:** Show the transformation process: Audio waves entering a machine -> turning into binary code -> exploding into colorful images -> morphing into video clips.
> 4.  **The Output:** Cascading film reels and digital screens showing rapid-fire video generation.
>
> **Atmosphere & Colors:**
> -   **Palette:** Electric Blue, Neon Pink, Toxic Green, and Deep Black. High contrast.
> -   **Lighting:** Dynamic strobe lights, neon signs, and volumetric fog.
> -   **Camera:** Fast-paced, zooming into nodes, flying along data cables, spinning around the AI avatar.
>
> **Editing:** Sync cuts perfectly with the beat. Use glitch effects, datamoshing, and digital noise transitions to emphasize the "AI" nature of the tool. Make it look like a premium, high-budget tech demo mixed with an underground rap video.
