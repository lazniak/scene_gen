# Wytyczne Promptu do Suno (Tutorial Hip-Hop)

Oto gotowe prompty (opisy), które możesz wkleić do Suno AI (w trybie prostym), aby wygenerować piosenkę o wtyczce Scene Gen.

## Opcja 1: Klasyczny Boom Bap (Luzacki Tutorial)
**Prompt:**
> A funny, step-by-step 90s boom bap hip-hop track acting as a tutorial for a ComfyUI plugin called "Scene Gen". The rapper explains how to connect audio, use Gemini for analysis, and Replicate for video generation. The vibe is educational but tongue-in-cheek, cool, and rhythmic. Mention features like "Asset Generation", "Montage", and "Stitching".

## Opcja 2: Nowoczesny Trap (Hype na Narzędzie)
**Prompt:**
> Upbeat trap song with heavy bass and auto-tune, explaining the "Scene Gen" AI tool. Lyrics describe taking an audio file and turning it into a full music video automatically. Fast flow, exciting, mentioning "Gemini 3 Pro" and "Video Models". It's a guide on how to be a lazy but genius creator.

## Opcja 3: Chill Hop (Relaksująca Instrukcja)
**Prompt:**
> Lo-fi hip hop beat with a smooth flow rap. The lyrics are a relaxed guide on using the Scene Gen node in ComfyUI. Step by step: Input Audio -> Gemini Analysis -> Style Definition -> Video Generation. "Sit back and let the AI do the work" vibe.

---

## Kluczowe elementy do zawarcia (jeśli piszesz własny prompt):
*   **Temat:** Wtyczka "Scene Gen" do ComfyUI.
*   **Funkcja:** Robi teledyski z muzyki (Audio -> Video).
*   **Technologia:** Używa Google Gemini (mózg) i Replicate (mięśnie/wideo).
*   **Styl:** Tutorial, ale "z przymrużeniem oka", zabawny, hip-hopowy.
